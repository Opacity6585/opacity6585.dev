Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 29, 2025

1. Information We Collect

The Bot may collect and store the following data:

  • Deleted messages, reactions, and edited messages (temporarily in memory for up to 2 hours)
  • Moderation history by user ID
  • User relationship data (e.g., for marriage system) by user ID
  • Reaction-based event data

2. Data Storage

Data is stored in JSON files on the server where the Bot is hosted. Temporary data is automatically deleted after approximately 2 hours.

3. Third-Party Services

The Bot may connect to third-party APIs, such as Last.fm, to enhance functionality. We do not control or assume responsibility for the data policies of these third parties.

4. Data Usage

Collected data is used solely for the operation and features of the Bot. We do not sell or share user data with any external parties.

5. Data Removal Requests

If you wish to have your data removed, contact us via Discord: @opacity6585. We will respond to reasonable requests in a timely manner.

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect stored data. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates will be posted on https://opacity6585.dev. Continued use of the Bot after updates means you accept the changes.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us via Discord: @opacity6585
